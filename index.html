Bio

Harshvardhan Uppaluru is an AI researcher, focused on co-design of intelligent algorithms and neuromorphic hardware for autonomous and resource-constrained systems. He previously served as Engineering Researcher I at The University of Alabama and was a Postdoctoral Fellow at University of South Alabama where he analyzed the performance of memristor-based neuromorphic systems for Edge AI. During his PhD at The University of Arizona, his work spanned multi-agent coordination and control.
