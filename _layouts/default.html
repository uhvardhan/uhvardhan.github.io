Harshvardhan Uppaluru

Do what you can, with what you have, where you are.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of South Alabama's Intelligent Multi-level Power-Aware Circuits and Systems (IMPACT) Lab, under the guidance of Prof. Jinhui Wang and Prof. Na Gong.

My academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Arizona, an M.E. in Robotics from the University of Maryland - College Park and a B.Tech. in Mechatronics from SRM University.

Outside academia, I serve as a certified USACUA and WICUA cricket referee, officiating in various leagues including Minor League Cricket. In my free time, I enjoy reading books (I am currently reading the Empyrean series), playing cricket, and supporting Arsenal FC.
